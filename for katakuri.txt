I see you have downloaded this.

do these instructions when u r working alone.
1) now go to source control located in the bar left side for your screen(or just do ctrl+shift+G).
2) A expanded sidebar will appear on left of the screen
3) hover your mouse against the second source control text
4) You will see three dot horizontal line icon, press it
5) Select pull, push in the appeared bar, and press sync.
6) now continue to do your work.
7) after completion, go to source control again (ctrl + shift + G)
8) enter empty blank space(not text) in the message text box
9) see the changes drop down menu below it, hover ur cursor on it
10) press the "+" icon
11) press commit above the changes drop down menu
12) press Sync changes.
13) now u can exit.
